Who We Are:

Chicago Music Magazine (CMM) was Founded in February 2012 by Chicago-based entrepreneur Shawn Kellner

Chicago Music Magazine is an online editorial music magazine and revolutionary new music discovery tool.

Our focus is to expose our readers to new music daily while enhancing their live music experience.

The company operates from its Chicago based headquarters inside the world famous Paragon Recording Studios located at: 820 W. Fulton Market, 4th Floor, Chicago, IL.

Chicago Music Magazine is the eyes and ears of a fan-forward inspiration.

The site’s traffic is driven by authentic content and a media-focused and driven attention to detail.

Chicago Music Magazine embraces and balances the necessity of journalistic integrity alongside the fundamental interests of its readers.

A fan-forward approach is taken in the creation of all content.
